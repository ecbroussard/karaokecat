Karaoke Venue Rating System

Here at Karaoke Cat reviews, we use a state-of-the-art patent pending rating system to scientifically assess the quality of each karaoke venue. Seriously. All of our ratings are peer reviewed.

Each karaoke venue is evaluated on a five-point system. This system includes the following categories:

  • Atmosphere
  • Food and Drink
  • Wait Time
  • Host/KJ
  • Song Selection

Atmosphere

A venue's atmosphere contributes greatly to its enjoyability. Is it a new and sparkly bar with eclectic decor, or is it a run-down shack where your feet stick to the floor? You're going to want to know!

Food and Drink

Some karaoke venues are bars who happen to sell snacks, and some are full-blown restaurants. Some have overpriced, watered-down drinks, and some have refreshing gourmet cocktails. I'll help you find a great place to sing and get some grub.

Wait Time

It's no fun to wait all night for your turn. The best karaoke venues are busy enough to have a fun and rowdy crowd but not so crowded that you have to wait four hours to sing one song. You need to find that sweet spot in the middle.

Host/KJ

The only thing worse than an overly enthusiastic KJ who tells really corny jokes is a bored, lifeless one who doesn't give two craps about his job. We'll tell you where the good ones are.

Song Selection

I've visited karaoke venues with amazing food, a great atmosphere, and a talented KJ only to find out they don't have any of my favorite songs. Believe me, it can ruin your whole night. I can tell you which venues have the best song selections.
